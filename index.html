FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is MultMatrix?

MultMatrix is an AI video subtitle tool for generating, translating, previewing, and downloading subtitles online.

Can I translate subtitles?

Yes. You can translate generated subtitles to prepare video content for audiences in another language.

Can I preview subtitles online?

Yes. The main tool lets you preview generated subtitles with video so you can check the result and timing before downloading. Subtitle track display in the demo above may depend on browser support.

What subtitle formats can I download?

You can download editable subtitle files such as VTT and SRT from the main subtitle tool.

Can I create burned-in subtitle videos?

Yes. MultMatrix can create video output with subtitles rendered into the picture, which is useful where separate subtitle tracks are not supported.

What video links are supported?

Supported links can vary by source and video availability. Open the main subtitle tool and provide your video link to check whether it can be processed.
