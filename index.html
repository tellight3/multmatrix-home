FAQ

Common questions.

What is MultMatrix?

MultMatrix is an AI video subtitle tool that generates subtitles, lets you preview them in sync, and provides editable subtitle files.

Can I preview subtitles online?

Yes. You can play your video with subtitles and review the timing before downloading.

Can I download VTT or SRT subtitle files?

Yes. The subtitle tool supports downloading editable VTT and SRT subtitle files.

Can I translate subtitles?

Yes. Subtitle translation helps prepare generated subtitles for multilingual video content.

What video links are supported?

Support varies by video source and availability. Paste your video link into the tool to check whether it can be processed.
